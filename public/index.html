ABOUT ME

Hi there! I’m Marine Mekhakyan. Based in LA and made in the vibrant city of Yerevan 🔆 I code, watch movies, cook, lie on the beach, pet animals, and recently started growing plants. I started coding not long after I moved to Los Angeles and have been building skills in front-end development and design. My favorite part in creating websites and web apps is understanding the project’s purpose and designing with specific audience in mind. It helps create a visual that speaks to the user and provides a simple and memorable experience. Being multilingual has its impact on how I see the world. More things make me curious and it drives the imagination which then translates in my work. It’s always eye-opening to meet people from different cultural backgrounds and hear their ideas and perspectives. It helps develop a better understanding of how our minds work and how to use our creative potential positively. Being able to bring these ideas to life through code is awesome.
I’m always excited to take on new projects and would love to hear from you, so feel free to get in touch!
